## 📄 License Recommendation

**MIT License** ✅

Why MIT for this project:
- It's a learning/educational project → maximum openness is appropriate
- Lets anyone fork, study, modify, and share freely
- No copyleft restrictions (unlike GPL) — contributors aren't obligated to open-source their modifications
- Industry standard for beginner/portfolio open-source projects
- Compatible with virtually every other license

Alternatives considered:
- **GPL v3** — good if you want all forks to stay open-source, but overkill for a learning project
- **Apache 2.0** — adds patent protection, unnecessary at this scale
- **Unlicense / CC0** — fully public domain, fine but MIT is more recognized

**Verdict: MIT is the right choice.** ✅
